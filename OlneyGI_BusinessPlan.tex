\section{Executive Summary}
% ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Olney GI Associates, LLC is a new gastroenterology specialty practice to be established in Olney, Maryland (Montgomery County), owned and led by \textbf{\ownername, \ownercredentials}, a Maryland-licensed Nurse Practitioner operating under the \textit{Maryland Nurse Practitioner Full Practice Authority Act (2015)}. The practice will operate a dual-revenue model combining a \textbf{private-pay concierge membership program} with \textbf{commercial insurance billing} beginning in Month~6 --- delivering immediate cash flow from Day~1 while building a growing insured patient panel.

\medskip
The model is designed to be the most financially resilient structure available to a new specialty practice. Membership fees provide predictable recurring revenue that bridges the commercial insurance credentialing period, and by the time insurance billing activates, the practice already has an established and loyal patient base. Montgomery County --- one of the wealthiest and most densely populated counties in the United States --- is ideally suited for this model.

\medskip
We are requesting an \textbf{SBA 7(a) loan of \$493,400} to fund all startup costs and a 12-month operating reserve. The practice is projected to reach break-even in Year~2 and generate net income exceeding \$500,000 by Year~3.

\section{Business Description}
% ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

\subsection{Practice Overview}

Olney GI Associates, LLC will be organized as a Limited Liability Company under Maryland law and will operate as a for-profit outpatient gastroenterology clinic in Olney, Maryland. The practice will offer comprehensive GI and hepatology services to adult patients in Montgomery County and surrounding areas.

Olney is a rapidly growing, affluent suburban community with no currently operating independent GI practice. The nearest GI specialty options require patients to travel to Rockville or Bethesda, creating a clear unmet demand that this practice is positioned to fill.

\subsection{The Dual Revenue Model}

\subsubsection{Stream 1 --- Concierge Membership (Active from Day 1)}

Patients enroll in an annual concierge membership at \textbf{\$2,400/year} (\$200/month). Membership provides premium \textit{access} benefits not covered by commercial insurance: same-day and next-day scheduling, 45--60 minute appointments, direct provider text/email messaging, unlimited telehealth follow-up, and a personalized annual GI wellness assessment. Insured members also have their clinical visits billed to their commercial insurance. The membership fee covers only the access and coordination services --- not clinical services billed to insurance.

\subsubsection{Stream 2 --- Commercial Insurance Billing (Active from Month 6)}

Beginning in Month~6, the practice accepts commercially insured patients on a standard appointment basis. These patients do not pay a membership fee. Target payers: CareFirst BlueCross BlueShield of Maryland, Aetna, United Healthcare, and Cigna.

\medskip
\textbf{Legal Note:} All membership agreements will be reviewed by a qualified healthcare attorney prior to launch to ensure compliance with applicable insurance regulations. This dual-billing structure is widely used by concierge practices nationwide and is legally sound when properly documented.

\section{Market Analysis}
% ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

\subsection{Market Overview}

Montgomery County, Maryland is the most populous county in Maryland with over \textbf{1.06 million residents} and a median household income exceeding \textbf{\$115,000} --- one of the highest of any county in the United States. The Olney submarket sits at the geographic center of the county along the Georgia Avenue corridor, with approximately 35,000 residents skewing toward middle-aged and older adults --- the highest utilizers of gastroenterology specialty services. There is currently \textbf{no independent GI practice with a primary Olney address}.

\subsection{Demand Drivers}

\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{GI specialist shortage:} Average new patient wait times of 4--6 weeks at hospital-affiliated practices in the county
  \item \textbf{Expanded screening eligibility:} USPSTF lowered colorectal cancer screening age to 45 in 2021, significantly expanding the eligible patient population
  \item \textbf{Aging population:} Montgomery County's 65+ population is growing rapidly; GI conditions are among the top reasons seniors seek specialty care
  \item \textbf{Affluent demographics:} One of the top U.S. markets for concierge medicine adoption
  \item \textbf{Underserved local market:} No independent GI practice serves Olney as a primary location
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Competitive Advantage}

\begin{itemize}
  \item Only GI practice with a primary Olney location --- zero direct competition in immediate market
  \item Concierge members receive same-day/next-day appointments --- impossible to replicate at hospital-system scale
  \item 45--60 minute visits vs.\ industry-standard 15 minutes --- dramatically superior patient experience
  \item Direct provider messaging --- a benefit unavailable at any competing local practice
  \item Commercial insurance accepted (Month~6+) --- accessible to the full insured population
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Pre-Opening: Founding Member Campaign}

Target: \textbf{30--50 founding members before Day~1} generating \$4,500--\$7,500 in recurring monthly revenue immediately.

\begin{itemize}
  \item Launch website with online concierge enrollment 60 days before opening
  \item Founding Member pricing: \textbf{\$1,800/year} (25\% discount vs.\ standard) --- creates urgency
  \item Targeted Facebook and Instagram ads to Olney, Sandy Spring, Ashton, and Rockville zip codes (ages 35--65, HHI \$80K+)
  \item Nextdoor community posts --- highly effective in Olney's community-oriented neighborhoods
  \item Personal outreach to PCPs in Olney and surrounding areas
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Referral Network Development}

\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Target:} 30--40 PCP relationships established in Months 1--6 across Olney, Gaithersburg, Rockville, and Silver Spring
  \item Monthly lunch-and-learn visits to PCP offices; referral pads distributed to all referring providers
  \item Prompt consultation reports returned to PCPs within 24--48 hours --- builds referral loyalty
  \item Urgent care centers in the Olney area as a steady pipeline for acute GI complaints
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Digital Presence}

\begin{itemize}
  \item Professional website with two clear pathways: ``Join as a Concierge Member'' and ``Book an Appointment (Insurance Accepted)''
  \item Google Business Profile optimized for: \textit{GI doctor Olney MD}, \textit{gastroenterologist near me Olney}, \textit{concierge GI Maryland}
  \item Healthgrades, Zocdoc, and Doximity profiles for both providers
  \item Monthly concierge member e-newsletter: GI wellness tips, practice updates --- drives retention and word-of-mouth referrals
\end{itemize}
